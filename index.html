At first glance, we’re no different than your typical salon: abuzz with blow dryers and coffee-fueled conversation. But we like to do things a little differently: We slow down. We listen to our clients, we remember their kids’ names like we do our friends’, and we never double-book. We only source non-toxic hair care and recycle 95% of our waste. Essentially, we made the salon experience equal parts luxurious and sustainable, so you can have your cake and eat it, too. Welcome to Hair Day!
